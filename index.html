International Exam Preparation Courses

Prepare for international dental licensing examinations with expert guidance and comprehensive study materials.

NBDE - United States

National Board Dental Examination

Two-part examination required for Indian dentists to practice in the U.S. Part I covers basic biomedical sciences, Part II focuses on clinical dentistry.

Learn more ‚Üí

NDEB - Canada

National Dental Examining Board of Canada

Equivalency process including Written Examination (WREB) and Objective Structured Clinical Examination (OSCE) to assess knowledge and clinical skills.

Learn more ‚Üí

ORE - United Kingdom

Overseas Registration Examination

Two-part examination: ORE Part 1 (written multiple-choice questions) and ORE Part 2 (practical OSCE) required to practice dentistry in the UK.

Learn more ‚Üí

ADC - Australia

Australian Dental Council Examination

Comprehensive assessment including written and practical examinations evaluating knowledge and clinical skills of foreign-trained dentists.

Learn more ‚Üí

NZDREX - New Zealand

New Zealand Dentists Registration Examination

Complete examination with written and clinical components required to practice dentistry in New Zealand.
